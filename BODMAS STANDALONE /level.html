BODMAS RULES

  • Brackets (B)- The highest level order is defined by anything contained in brackets.
  • ORDER (O) - Order of operations - when precedence is equal is generally from left to right.
  • MULTIPLICATION and DIVISION rank equally , so you go from left to right in the equation, doing each operation in the order in which it appears.
  • Let us look at   Example :   (18 / 6) *( 8 / 4 )
    You just work from left to right. Eighteen over six is three, times eight is twenty four, divided by four is 6.
  • ADDITION and SUBTRACTION : again carry equal weight and you're supposed to go from left to right.
  • For Example :    40-12+22 will give the answer as 50.
